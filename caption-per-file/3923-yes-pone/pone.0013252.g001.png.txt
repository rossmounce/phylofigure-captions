Phylogenic trees of six silkworm coatomer subunits.Six phylogenic trees of α-COP (COPA) (A), β-COP (COPB) (B), β′-COP (COPB2) (C), δ-COP (COPD) (D), ε-COP (COPE) (E), and ζ-COP (COPZ) (F) are shown. Each tree contains several coatomer homologues from different organisms and is arbitrarily rooted using coatomers from S. cerevisiae. The red borders indicate the silkworm coatomer subunits. Bm, Bombyx mori; Ce, Caenorhabditis elegans; Dm, Drosophila melanogaster; Dr, Danio rerio; Hs, Homo sapiens; Mm, Mus musculus; Rn, Rattus norvegicus; and Sc, Saccharomyces cerevisiae."
