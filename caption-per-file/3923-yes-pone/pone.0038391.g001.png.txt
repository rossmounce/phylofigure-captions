Unrooted phylogenetic tree of all lepidopteran odorant receptors within Genbank as of 8 November, 2010.The neighbour joining tree was constructed from dayhoff amino acid distances. The positions of OR1, OR2 (Orco), and OR3 from Epiphyas postvittana are indicated with arrows (EpOR1, EpOR2, and EpOR3), while the Orco and sex pheromone receptor clades are highlighted by semicircles."
