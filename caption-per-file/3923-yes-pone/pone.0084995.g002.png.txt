Placement of Xanthomonas translucens isolate DAR61454 in the Xanthomonas genus using phylogenetic analysis of the atpD gene nucleotide sequence.Phylogenetic analysis was carried out at www.phylogeny.fr as detailed in the methods. Numbers of branches represent branch support based on approximate likelihood ratio testing. Monocot infecting or associated xanthomonads are shaded in grey. All other xanthomonads are associated with dicot species."
