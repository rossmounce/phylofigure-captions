Phylogenies of Myctophidae reconstructed from (A) Paxton et al.[43], (B) Poulsen et al. [44]. The red branches indicate the main differences between the two phylogenies. Branch lengths are arbitrarily ultrametricized on the figure. In A, the numbers identify the different tribes of Paxton et al. [43], 1. Diaphini, C. Notoscopelini, D-E-F. Lampanyctini, G. Electronini, H. Myctophini, I. Myctophini (cycloid-species-group), J. Myctophini (ctenoid-species group)+Gonichthyini."
