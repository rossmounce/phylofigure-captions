Cladogram depicting the phylogenetic relationships of the family Liolaemidae, constructed joining recent partial phylogenies for each of the clades included (called a Metatree). Each terminal, with the exception of Ctenoblepharys represent several species: chiliensis group, 86 species; boulengeri group, 40 species; montanus group, 9 species, lineomaculatus group, 12 species."
