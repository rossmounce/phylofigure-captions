Bayesian consensus phylogeny of E. sasakii.Node support is indicated by posterior probabilities, and is given where >80. The upper (green), central (red), and lower (blue) clade represent specimens isolated from R. sariuoni, T. japonica, and E. kuwanai/E. gigiantea, respectively. First two letters of terminal name indicate sampling locality, where QH = Qinhai, SD = Shandong, BJ = Beijing, SH = Shaanxi, HJ = Heilongjiang, JL = Jilin, JS = Jiangsu, HN = Henan, SX = Shanxi."
