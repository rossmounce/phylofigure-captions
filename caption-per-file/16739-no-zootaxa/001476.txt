"FIGURE 2. Adult female holotype of Cyrtodactylus kimberleyensis sp. nov. (WAM R164144). Scale bar = 10 mm.Diagnosis. A small sized Cyrtodactylus (female gravid at SVL of 45 mm); body slender; limbs and digits relatively long, slender; partly regenerated tail longer than SVL; one pair of greatly enlarged postmental scales in contact with one another behind mental, a smaller pair of enlarged chin shields (second postmentals) lateral to these;"
