"FIGURE 6. Photographs of dorsal skin structure in preserved specimens of (a) Gephyromantis decaryi (ZSM 2497/2007; fieldnumber ZCMV 5223), (b) G. verrucosus (ZSM 2462/2007; ZCMV 5459), (c) G. hintelmannae (holotype ZSM 2500/2007;ZCMV 5230), and (d) G. sp. 7 (ZSM 2492/2007; ZCMV 5485). Note continuous dorsolateral ridges in G. decaryi, discontinuous ridges and elongated tubercles in G. verrucosus, and relatively smooth skin with weakly expressed ridges in G. hintelmannae and G. sp. 7. Scale bars represent 1 mm."
