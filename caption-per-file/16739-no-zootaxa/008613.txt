"FIGURE 72. C. stenocephalus De Lotto. Paratype. For explanation of labels, see p. 9; X = setal socket. Notes: (i) stigmatic setae in acompact group and all sharply conical; (ii) dorsum apparently without clear areas; (iii) dorsal setae long and spinose; (iv) caudal process particularly large and almost mid-dorsal; (v) loculate microducts of complex-type; (vi) ventral tubular ducts restricted to anogenital folds, and(vii) antennae with 7 or 8 segments."
