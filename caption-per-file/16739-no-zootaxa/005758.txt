"FIGURE 9. Distribution of Dorcadion axillare. (VD) ‘Valea lui David’, (Mz) Mârzeşti, (Ch) Chirceşti, (Mc) Măcin, (Bb)Babadag, (It) Istria, (Mg) Mangalia, (Hg) Hagieni, (Ot) Oltina, (EB) Esechioi and Bugeac, (VB) Valea Beiului. Black rectangles: D. a. moldavicum, black dots: D. a. axillare. The distribution in Bulgaria is based on the locality data published by Kantardjieva-Minkova (1934) and Minkova (1961), and on specimens in first author’s collection and public collections."
