"FIGURES 7A–B. Mean abundance of Arauchemus gen. nov. species at three habitat types in Araucaria Forest areas at PróMata, São Francisco de Paula, RS, Brazil, from October 2000 to May 2002. PRI, primary forest habitats; SEC, secondary forest habitats; PIN, Pinus silviculture habitats. Error bars representing standard errors of mean."
